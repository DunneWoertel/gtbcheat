How to use this tool efficiently

  • 1. Select the number of words to guess
  • 2. Enter the length of each word in the textboxes
  • 3. Enter the hints in the textboxes as they appear in the game.
    Words that matches the hints and the settings will be shown in the list.
  • 4. Try all the words in the list and you'll end up guessing what it is.
    You can also click on the word to copy it to the clipboard.

  • Keep in mind that not all words are known, so it might not be there. In this case, I invite you to submit a missing word. If you want to report a bug or suggest other servers and new features, you can send me feedback.
